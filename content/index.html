Martian Cities

It's time to build cities on Mars.

Read the Martian Cities Manifesto

Why Mars?

It's my firm belief that Mars will be the second home of humanity, the place where we learn to settle the rest of the solar system. I'm not alone in this, either; companies, organizations, and lots of individuals are all working toward permanent settlements on Mars.

More about settling Mars »

Why cities?

It might seem like jumping the gun to talk about building cities on Mars when the first human has yet to set foot there. However, Mars exploration is heating up quickly, and I believe that city planning should be firmly in mind from the moment the first lander arrives.
